Instructions:


The game will take place over three rounds. During each round, you will roll two dice and determine which was the higher score by adding the two dice together

If you roll the same number with both dice, your current score is multiplied by two

However, if you roll a one with either of your dice, your current score is set to zero for that round

Whichever player has the highest score at the end of round three is the winner
